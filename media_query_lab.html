Kittens are young cats, typically under one year of age, known for their playful and curious nature. They are small, adorable creatures with fluffy fur, large eyes, and tiny whiskers. Kittens are known for their energetic and mischievous behavior, often darting around the house, pouncing on toys, and exploring their surroundings with boundless curiosity. These young felines are highly social animals and often form strong bonds with their human caregivers. They enjoy cuddling and being petted, and they often purr loudly to express their contentment. Kittens are also known for their agility and flexibility, often showcasing impressive acrobatic feats as they play and explore.
